Hockey

Hockey is a sport in which two teams play against each other by trying to manoeuvre a ball or a puck into the opponent's goal using a hockey stick. There are many types of hockey such as bandy, field hockey, ice hockey and rink hockey.

In most of the world, the term hockey by itself refers to field hockey.

Field Hockey

Field hockey is played on gravel, natural grass, or sand-based or water-based artificial turf, with a small, hard ball.The game is popular among both males and females in many parts of the world.

The governing body is the 126-member International Hockey Federation (FIH).Field hockey is the national sport of Pakistan.

Ice Hockey

Ice hockey is played between two teams of skaters on a large flat area of ice.This puck is often frozen before high-level games to decrease the amount of bouncing and friction on the ice.

The governing body of international play is the 77-member International Ice Hockey Federation (IIHF). Ice hockey is the national sport of Latvia and the national winter sport of Canada.
